PWA Weather

A scalable, native comparable Progressive Web App

My final year research project is targeted at the performance optimisation of Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), such that they offer a competitive and scalable alternative to existing native apps. To collect primary research, I have developed a weather forecasting PWA. This is being used to test potential optimisations and gain user feedback on PWA performance compared to native alternatives. Views on optimisation complexity from industry professionals are also being collected to ensure the scalability of the application.
